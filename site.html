Our Services

Hi there My name is Elena, and I currently reside in Inverkeithing, Fife. With a lifelong passion for caring for cats and dogs, I have transitioned this dedication into a professional service, providing pet care within the comfort of their home environment. I offer flexible visiting options, ranging from once daily visits to multiple visits throughout the day, and I am also available for overnight stays. I am willing to travel to your location in Edinburgh, Fife, Glasgow, Perth, and all areas in between, ensuring that I can meet both you and your beloved pets. During our meeting, I would appreciate your guidance on the specifics of your pet's routine and environment. Please feel free to reach out to me in the comments section, and I will confirm my availability and provide you with a tailored quote. Please visit the testimonial section to see what the other clients have said. Best regards, Elena
